NYU Shanghai and NYU Stern Partnership

NYU Shanghai is the third degree-granting campus of New York University and the first sino-U.S. joint venture university in China. With its unique platform for research and study, NYU Shanghai is pioneering new models for education and global engagement in the 21st Century. NYU Stern is ranked among the top business schools in the world for its strength in finance and international business. Together, NYU Shanghai and NYU Stern are partnering to offer the premier master’s programs in the world that prepare students for China and Asia-focused business careers.
